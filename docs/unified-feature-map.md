# Unified Feature Map

This document maps external OSINT repositories to implemented capabilities in Nexus MENA OSINT.

## Core intelligence engines

- `smicallef/spiderfoot`: module-driven collection and correlation architecture.
- `laramies/theHarvester`: domain/email/subdomain intelligence patterns.
- `s0md3v/Photon`: fast crawl and endpoint discovery model.
- `Lissy93/web-check`: website intelligence panel and risk surfacing.
- `alpkeskin/mosint`: email-centric enrichment ideas.

## Social identity and account discovery

- `sherlock-project/sherlock`: username hunting across social surfaces.
- `qeeqbox/social-analyzer`: profile confidence and multi-network matching.
- `Alfredredbird/tookie-osint` + `Lucksi/Mr.Holmes`: multi-source social probes.

## Platform packs

- `The-Osint-Toolbox/Telegram-OSINT` and `ItIsMeCall911/Awesome-Telegram-OSINT`: Telegram domain coverage.
- `Datalux/Osintgram` + `sc1341/InstagramOSINT`: Instagram metadata and profile intelligence.
- `jasperan/whatsapp-osint`: constrained signal model for authorized investigations.

## Source catalogs and automation

- `jivoi/awesome-osint`, `lockfale/OSINT-Framework`, `Ph055a/OSINT_Collection`, `cipher387/API-s-for-OSINT`: source registry and connector planning.
- `langchain-ai/social-media-agent`: analyst-assistant workflow patterns.

## Exclusions and constraints

- Repositories not related to OSINT product scope are not integrated.
- Offensive or abuse-prone behavior is excluded from runtime features.
- All collection remains OSINT-compliant and evidence traceable.
